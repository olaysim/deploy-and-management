What are cookies?

A cookie be a wee text file that a website saves on yer 'puter or mobile device when ye visit th' site. It enables th' website t' remember yer actions 'n preferences (such as login, language, font size 'n other display preferences) o'er a period o' time, so ye don't 'ave t' keep re-boardin' them whenever ye come back t' th' site or browse from one page t' another.
